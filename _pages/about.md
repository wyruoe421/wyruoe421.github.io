Hi, I'm **Yiruo Wang**. 

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Management at *Cardiff University*, funded by the [China Scholarship Concil](https://www.csc.edu.cn).  
My research focuses on investigating **inventory record inaccuracies**, with support from **ECR Losss**.

Previously, I completed an **Logistics Engineering** at *Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications*, where I worked on the joint pricing and decision for perishable products. My undergraduate degree was in **Postal Engineering** from *Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications*.
